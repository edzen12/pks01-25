Kyrgyzstan, or Kyrgyzstan (kirg. Kyrgyzstan [qɯrʁɯzˈstɑn]), officially — Kyrgyz Republic[c] (kirg. The Kyrgyz Republic (abbreviation — KR[d]) is a state in Central Asia, located in the western and central parts of the Tien Shan mountain system and the Pamir-Alai.
